Every Little Bit Counts

Uganda is one of the poorest countries on the African continent and in the world. A fourth of the world’s malnourished children currently live in Uganda and the long-term damages that the children endure due to this environment are irreversible, from contracting infections to leading a life that results in a higher mortality rate.

With your support, we at Gift Angels Foundation, will be able to provide the necessary services and items to fight against the malnourishment problem in Uganda’s youth. Donation funds are used to provide Uganda’s youth with necessities, such as food, shelter, medical care, and access to education.

We at Gift Angels strive to be completely transparent with all donors on where and how donated funds are used. We believe every child deserves a safe and nurturing environment as they grow, but sadly, in Uganda, the harsh environment doesn’t allow for safety or nourishment. We appreciate and thank all donors. Please know your contribution makes a difference in someone’s life and brings us closer to our goals.

Initiatives

NUTRITION & HEALTH

Due to the issue of malnourishment in Uganda, your support will allow us to provide the proper health education and necessities, such as food and water

EDUCATION

We aim to provide educational services from health education, to prevent the spread of HIV/AIDS, to reduce the level of illiteracy for the youth through the basic needs of an educational system.

SUPPORT FOR HIV POSITIVE

The risk of contracting HIV/AIDS within the underserved Uganda communities is quite high. We plan to fight the risk by providing awareness campaigns and necessary medical supplies to fight the disease.

WATER & SANITATION

Water and sanitation are essential needs for all living beings, but in Uganda, poor hygiene education has left many children at risk of getting ill. We want to change the way children access drinking water and provide the proper education on hygiene/sanitation.

AGRICULTURE

Within Uganda, agriculture is the source of providing income to support oneself and their families. With your help, quality crops will be purchased to meet the basic needs of poverty-stricken individuals.

COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT

Poverty-stricken communities in Uganda face receiving the basic needs needed to live a healthy life. Your contribution will help in developing sanitation, health, infrastructure development, disaster readiness, etc.

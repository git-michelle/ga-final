BRING A SMILE TO A CHILD

Spreading Joy in Karamoja

Our Bring a Smile project aims to address issues that affect orphans and vulnerable children in Uganda. Currently, we are focusing on the Karamoja community as there are a high number of youths dealing with domestic violence and non-consensual marriages.

The standard of living in Karamoja has been improving gradually, but that gradual improvement has not been helpful to the Karamojong youths. With Bring a Smile To A Child, we hope to raise awareness about these specific issues and to provide the necessary services and support.

Touring Karamoja

Our goal for this initiative is to provide every young Karamojong the tools to enjoy quality education and a healthy lifestyle. Touring Karamoja allows us to raise funds for our Bring a Smile project and to fight critical issues. Some of the key components of Touring Karamoja are as follows:

Raising awareness of sexual and reproductive health issues in Karamoja, such as young women acquiring HIV/AIDS on the streets of Kampala.

Develop and run an awareness campaign to fight, with the hopes to completely end, domestic and gender-based violence.

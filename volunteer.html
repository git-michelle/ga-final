With millions of children being deprived of their basic rights to the most essential human needs, we need your help in providing the necessary services to fight against the issues at hand in Uganda.

Why Volunteer

With the number of projects we run throughout the year, we rely on the amazing work that our dedicated and enthusiastic core of volunteers provide to help Gift Angels Foundation’s overall mission to assist the youth of Uganda.

By volunteering with us, not only do you make a huge difference in the lives of children and their families in Uganda, but you help us learn how to continue to grow as a non-profit organization and we appreciate everyone that gives their time to support our mission!

As a volunteer, you will be supporting Gift Angels Foundation’s mission through a range of projects, such as, fundraising programs, awareness campaigns, marketing campaigns, and assisting in administrative tasks. All the volunteer work that is completed allows for Gift Angels Foundation to have the necessary tools to continue to push and fight against the issues at hand.

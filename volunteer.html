Volunteers are one of our most valuable resources and we appreciate their motivation and commitment. You can learn more about our current volunteer opportunities by contacting us or reading more below.

Why Volunteer

We rely on the amazing work that our dedicated and enthusiastic core of volunteers provide to help Gift Angels Foundation’s overall mission to empower the women and children of Uganda.

By volunteering with us, not only do you make a huge difference in the lives of Ugandan youth, but you help us learn how to continue to grow as a non-profit organization! Volunteering can be a meaningful experience for you and be an opportunity to meet new people, and share or enhance your own skills.

As a volunteer, you will be supporting Gift Angels Foundation’s mission through a range of activities that include fundraising programs, awareness campaigns, social media engagement, and assisting in administrative tasks. All the volunteer work that is completed allows Gift Angels Foundation to have the necessary tools to continue to grow our Bring a Smile to a Child and Mothers of Gold programs.
